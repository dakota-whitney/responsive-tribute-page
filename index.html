I am Iron Man

A Tribute to Tony Stark

Tony Stark was a genius, billionair, playboy, philanthropist and original member of the first Avengers team. He bravely sacrificed himself to undo the Decimation caused by the mad titan Thanos.


Here's a list of Tony Stark's incredible achievements:

  • Took over his father's weapon manufacturing empire at the age of 18
  • Escaped imprisonment by the Middle Eastern mercenary group, The 10 Rings, by using salvaged Jericho missile parts to build body-sized armor
  • Stopped his colleague Obidiah Stain, aka the Iron Monger, from mass producing and weaponizing his armor
  • Stopped Whiplash from murdering attendees of the Stark Expo in 2010
  • Fought alongside the Avengers during the famous Battle of New York, and redirected a nuclear missile into the void of space
  • Stopped the Mandarin from terrorizing America and recruiting people for his deadly Extremis experiments
  • Fought alongside the Avengers to stop the AI rogue Ultron from exterminating the human race with a city-turned-meteor
  • Helped in turning the Avengers into a government-owned resource
  • Helped train the now full fledged Avenger, Spider-Man
  • Finally, he sacrificed himself to undo the murdering of half the universe by Thanos
